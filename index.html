Information about ARJUN GARDENS

Learn what makes this garden a special mini picnic destination, and the purpose behind its creation â€” a green, clean space dedicated to environmental care.

ARJUN GARDENS is a beautifully developed garden that serves as a cosy mini picnic spot, filled with lush greenery, walking paths, and peaceful corners.

The property is created and maintained by Mr. Arjun Renjiv starting from the year 2030. His vision is to offer a natural environment where people can relax, unwind, and feel closer to nature.

Every area of the garden is built with the idea of green and clean surroundings. It is not just a picnic spot, but also a reminder of the importance of environment protection.

Visitors are encouraged to respect nature, avoid littering, and participate in keeping the space clean and eco-friendly.
